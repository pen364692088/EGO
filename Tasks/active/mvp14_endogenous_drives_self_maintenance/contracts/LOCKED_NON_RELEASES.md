# WP9 Locked Non-Releases

## 当前仍然不放开

- live autonomy
- OpenEmotion direct reply authority
- broader transport claims
- drive bypass of Governor
- drive-authored tool execution
- drive-authored transport execution
- unrestricted self-modification
- authority escalation from internal pressure alone

## Interpretive Rule

`WP8` 的 controlled `E5` 只证明：
- formal self-model owner 已稳定
- controlled observation 已稳定

它不证明：
- live autonomous speaking
- transport maturity
- direct reply authority transfer
- `WP9` drives 可以越过宿主治理

## Release Boundary

在 `WP9` 第一刀完成后，允许宣称的只有：
- authority / contract 已冻结
- capability ownership 已冻结
- implementation may start later

不允许宣称：
- `MVP14` 已接主链
- `MVP14` 已启用
- `MVP14` 已生效
