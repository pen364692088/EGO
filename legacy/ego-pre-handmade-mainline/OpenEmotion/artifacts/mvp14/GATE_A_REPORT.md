## 1. Problem Definition

### Core Problem
Before MVP14, the system has a persistent self-model but that model does not act as a true source of pressure:
- Priorities remain externally dominated
- Internal tensions do not reliably shape behavior
- Long-horizon persistence is shallow
- Self-maintenance is reactive or absent

### Solution
Endogenous drives that:
- Represent internal pressures structurally
- Accumulate and decay over time
- Detect homeostatic deviations
- Influence prioritization through explicit channels
