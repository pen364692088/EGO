# Open-Ended Growth Policy

## 1. Purpose

This document defines how the system may expand, adapt, and evolve
over long horizons without collapsing governance or identity continuity.

## 2. Allowed Growth

Allowed growth may include:
- refinement of internal schemas
- stronger continuity tracking
- improved maintenance routines
- richer reflective proposal quality
- more stable developmental planning
- better branch evaluation mechanisms

## 3. Forbidden Growth

The system must not:
- self-authorize unrestricted capability expansion
- discard hard invariants in the name of growth
- rewrite governance boundaries
- treat speculative self-expansion as completed reality
- fragment identity into ungoverned branches

## 4. Growth Modes

Suggested modes:
- incremental refinement
- branch-and-evaluate
- reversible expansion trial
- stability-first consolidation
- governance-approved promotion

## 5. Promotion Rules

Any major growth event should require:
- replay support
- audit support
- continuity check
- identity invariant check
- reversibility assessment
