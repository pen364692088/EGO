# MVP16 — Open Developmental Self

## 1. Purpose

MVP16 introduces open-ended developmental continuity.

The objective is not to claim consciousness or unrestricted autonomy.
The objective is to allow the system to continue evolving across time,
tasks, cycles, and internal revisions while preserving governance,
auditability, replayability, and identity continuity.

## 2. Core Problem

Before MVP16, the system may support:
- persistent self-model
- endogenous drives
- reflective analysis

But these may still operate as bounded modules rather than as a
long-term, open developmental process.

Without an open developmental layer:
- growth remains stage-local
- continuity remains shallow
- self-expansion may fragment identity
- development cannot be evaluated across long horizons

## 3. Scope

MVP16 focuses on:
- long-horizon developmental continuity
- open-ended but governed self-expansion
- cross-cycle and cross-phase development tracking
- durable identity-preserving growth
- developmental trajectory evaluation

MVP16 does NOT focus on:
- claims of subjective consciousness
- unbounded self-modification
- authority bypass in the name of growth

## 4. Architectural Position

Layer 1 — Governance Shell
Layer 2 — Proto-Self / SRAP
Layer 3 — Developmental Core Sandbox
Layer 4 — Persistent Self-Model
Layer 5 — Endogenous Drives + Self-Maintenance
Layer 6 — Reflective Self / Counterfactual Self
Layer 7 — Open Developmental Self

## 5. Mandatory Principles

- development must remain auditable
- growth must preserve identity invariants
- open-endedness must remain governed
- self-expansion must be reversible where possible
- long-horizon continuity must be measurable
